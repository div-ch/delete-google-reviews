Policy Violations

Why Platforms Remove Reviews

Reviews can be removed when they violate a platform's content policies. We identify these violations and build the case that gets them taken down.

Fake or Incentivized Reviews
Reviews from people who never used your service, paid review farms, or reviews posted in exchange for discounts violate every major platform's policies.
Conflict of Interest
Reviews left by current or former employees, competitors, or individuals with a personal dispute and financial motive are flagged as conflicts of interest.
Spam and Off-Topic Content
Reviews advertising other businesses, containing irrelevant information, or posting the same review across multiple locations are classified as spam.
Hate Speech and Personal Attacks
Reviews that contain personal insults, slurs, threats, or discriminatory language directed at individuals violate community standards and terms of service.
Mistaken Business Identity
Reviews clearly intended for a different business location or a similarly named competitor are removable when the misidentification can be documented.
Legal and Privacy Violations
Reviews disclosing confidential information, making defamatory claims, or violating court orders may qualify for expedited removal under legal grounds.
